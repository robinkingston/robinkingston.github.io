What We Offer

Services tailored to your cat's needs

Every cat is different. Robin provides personalised care to keep them content, safe, and purring while you're away.

🏠

Daily Home Visits

One or two daily visits to feed, water, and spend quality time with your cats. Litter trays cleaned, curtains managed, and your home looked after.

💊

Medication & Special Care

Experienced with tablets, liquids, and special dietary needs. Your cat's health requirements are in safe, capable hands.

📷

Photo Updates

Regular photo and text updates so you can see your cat is happy, healthy, and well looked after. Holiday peace of mind guaranteed.

Keys kept securely. Lights rotated, curtains managed, and post brought in to give your home a lived-in look.

About Robin

A genuine love for cats

Robin is a dedicated, experienced cat sitter who understands that your cat is family. With years of caring for cats of all breeds and temperaments, Robin provides a reliable, compassionate service you can trust.

Cats are happiest in their own environment — familiar sights, sounds, and smells. That's why Cats at Home brings the care to them. No stressful catteries, no unfamiliar surroundings.
